A Little More About Me.

I am an 80s baby. Also a Sagittarius. I was born in Queens, New York City, but spent most of my childhood growing up in Port-au-Prince, Haiti. The most perfect place I have ever known. I moved to the New York the day Prince Diana died, August 31, 1997. The rest is history.

Most of my life was spent in New York. I now life in Charlotte, North Carolina with my family. I am a proud father to the most amazing human being I've ever known, my son, Carter.

I am also an introvert with extroverted tendencies. I love music. I love animals. I love sports.
